🎓 WHAT IS A PWA? (Simple Explanation)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PWA = Progressive Web App

Think of it as:
- Your website dressed up as a mobile app
- Users can install it on their phone home screen
- It works offline (when cached)
- Looks and feels like a native app
- No need for Play Store (can install directly)

Benefits:
✅ One codebase for web + mobile
✅ Automatic updates (just update your website)
✅ Works on ALL platforms (Android, iOS, Desktop)
✅ Smaller size than native apps
✅ No Play Store approval needed (for direct install)
✅ Users always have the latest version

APK = Android Package
- Native Android app format
- Can be installed directly on Android
- Can be published to Google Play Store
- Built FROM your PWA

❓ FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Do I need to pay for this?
A: No! PWA Builder is FREE. You only pay if you want to publish on Google
   Play Store ($25 one-time developer fee).

Q: Will this work on iPhone?
A: Yes! PWAs work on iPhone through Safari. Users can "Add to Home Screen".
   However, APK files are Android-only.

Q: Do I need to rebuild APK when I update my website?
A: No! The APK is just a wrapper. When you update your website, the app
   automatically shows the updates. Only rebuild if you want a new version
   number or icon change.

Q: Can I publish this to Play Store?
A: Yes! You can submit the APK to Google Play Store. You'll need a Google
   Play Developer account ($25 one-time).

Q: What if my users don't have Google Play?
A: Users can install the APK directly (sideloading). Just share the APK
   file with them via email, USB, or cloud storage.

Q: Is this a real app or just a website?
A: It's a hybrid! It's your website packaged as an app. It looks and works
   like a native app, but the content comes from your website.

Q: Will it work offline?
A: Yes, if you've added the PWA code. The service worker caches pages so
   users can access previously loaded pages offline.

Q: How big is the APK?
A: Very small! Usually 1-5 MB because it's just a wrapper. The actual
   content loads from your website.
